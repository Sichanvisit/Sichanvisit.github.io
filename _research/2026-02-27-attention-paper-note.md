## Key points
- Self-Attention replaces recurrent computation for sequence modeling.
- Parallelizable architecture improves training efficiency.
- Positional encoding injects order information.

## Practical notes
- Sequence length and attention heads require memory/performance trade-off tuning.
- Data quality and preprocessing strongly affect output stability.
